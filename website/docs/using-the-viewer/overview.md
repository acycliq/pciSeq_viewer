# Using the Viewer

Once a dataset is loaded, the viewer gives you several coordinated ways to
explore your spatial transcriptomics results in 3D.

:::note[Scaffold page]

The pages in this section are stubs seeded from the project structure. Fill each
one in with screenshots/GIFs and step-by-step usage. Capture images into
`static/img/` and reference them as `/img/your-image.png`.

:::

## What you can do

- **[3D voxel viewer](./voxel-viewer)** — navigate cells, spots and boundaries across z-planes.
- **[Selection tool](./selection-tool)** — lasso/box-select regions and inspect their contents.
- **[Genes & cells tables](./genes-and-cells)** — searchable, sortable data tables linked to the view.
- **[Diagnostics](./diagnostics)** — explore the per-run diagnostics database.
